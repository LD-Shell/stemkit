Digitization protocol

  1. Calibrate: Click "Set X1", then click the known point on the X-axis. Type its value. Repeat for X2, Y1, Y2.
  2. Add datasets: Use the "+" button to create distinct curves. Pick colors to match the literature plot.
  3. Extract: Use Manual to click points, or hold/drag. Use Auto-extract to click a colored line for instant tracing.
  4. Clean up: Select Erase to brush over and remove erroneous points from the active dataset.
